About

Who I Am

I'm a 23-year-old Data Science student with a strong business mindset and a clear ambition: to build a career at the intersection of analytics and investing. I'm currently completing a Master's in Business Analytics and Data Science, where I combine technical curiosity with a growing passion for private equity.

My path reflects both resilience and self-awareness. I started in general engineering, recognized the misalignment, and pivoted into business engineering in Brussels — transferring prior coursework and graduating in two years instead of three. That transition led me to finance, which evolved into a broader commitment to financial literacy, capital allocation, and understanding how markets connect to real companies.

That interest crystallized during a private equity back-office internship, where I had close exposure to the deal environment. It confirmed two things: private equity is the long-term direction I want to pursue, and I thrive in roles closer to analysis, decisions, and the businesses themselves.

"To be careful whose advice you buy, but patient with those who supply it."
